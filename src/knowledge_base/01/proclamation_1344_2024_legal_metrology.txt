A PROCLAMATION ISSUED TO PROVIDE 
FOR LEGAL METROLOGY SYSTEM 
 
WHEREAS, it has been found necessary to protect 
and keep the interest, health and safety of society 
and animals as well as the environment by 
ensuring the verification of measurements and 
measuring 
instruments 
used 
in 
relation 
to 
commercial 
transactions, 
health, 
safety 
and 
environmental protection; 
 
WHEREAS, it has been found necessary to create 
a system to control the labeling and net quantity of 
pre-packaged products, before and after entering 
into the market, thereby enabling the consumers to 
get the right product and service for the price they 
pay;

environment for the executive bodies to conduct 
their regulatory activities;

framework in order to avoid trade barriers and 
strengthen regional integration;

9. Handling of Measuring Instruments 
1/ The reading display of a measuring 
instruments used for commercial purpose 
shall be clearly  shown to the seller and the 
buyer. 
2/ Any measuring instrument shall be kept in a 
manner that is not easily tampered with by 
any person or fraudulent action that will 
distort its measurement result. 
 
3/ User manuals and label affixed on 
measuring instruments shall be written in 
Amharic and English languages or at least  
in  one of them.

instrument 
without

such measuring instrument or part thereof.

particulars of the pattern of the measuring 
instrument 
or 
part 
there 
of

assembling, 
exporting, 
importing or selling
