1.  Short Title 
This Proclamation may be cited as the 
“International Development Association 
Loan Agreement for financing Financial 
Sector Strengthening Project Ratification 
Proclamation No. 1366/2025”. 
 
2.  Ratification of the Agreement 
 
The Loan Agreement Credit Number 7673-
ET signed on the on the 31th day of 
December 2024 between the Federal 
Democratic Republic of Ethiopia and 
International Development Association is 
hereby ratified.     
 
3.  Power of the Ministry of Finance 
The Ministry of Finance is hereby 
empowered to undertake all acts necessary 
for the utilization of the Loan an equivalent 
to Five Hundred and Twenty-Five Million 
Seven Hundred Thousand Special Drawing 
Rights (SDR 525,700,000) in accordance 
with the terms and conditions set forth in 
the loan Agreement. 
 
4. Effective Date 
This Proclamation shall enter into force 
upon its publication in the Federal Negarit 
Gazette. 
 
Done at Addis Ababa, On this 6th Day of 
February, 2025 
 
 
 TAYE    ATSKE-SELASSIE 
 
PRESIDENT OF THE FEDERAL 
DEMOCRATIC REPUBLIC OF 
ETHIOPIA
